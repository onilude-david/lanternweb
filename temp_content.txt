Preface
Steps  to  Quantitative Reasoning  is  a  collection  of  carefully  graded  books  
designed to put the pupils through the skills needed for quantitative reasoning.
It is hoped that at the end of the series, the pupils who use it would have a basic 
understanding	in	tackling	any	   past	   question	papers	on	  Quantitative	Aptitude,	
and	   that	   it	 would	guide	the	  child	    step-by-step	to	 a	 brilliant	performance	in	 the

careful attention and reasoning, the series impact, gradually, these skills to 
children.

the series.

the	   pupils’	activities	and	   to	  prepare	them	for	  Universal	Basic	Education	
examinations.
